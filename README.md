InkCode (Black & White Landing Page)
===================================

A minimal landing page inspired by Cursor, built in pure HTML/CSS/JS. Strict black & white aesthetic, English-only copy.

Usage
-----

- Open `index.html` directly in your browser, or serve the folder with any static server.
- Files:
  - `index.html` — structure and content
  - `styles.css` — theme and layout (black & white only)
  - `script.js` — small interactions (mobile nav, smooth scroll)

Customize
---------

- Update copy in `index.html` to match your product.
- Extend styles in `styles.css` — keep to grayscale for the requested style.
